How to use this drawing tool:

If your day was a shape and a color, what would it be?

Click "drawing mode", pick a color, and draw a symbol to immortalize the feeling of today (or any other day, use the calendar input to choose). You can also just leave a little dot if you don't feel like coming up with a symbol - the app just needs something drawn to document a day. Click "save"!

Once you're done drawing, you can switch back to 'viewing mode'. Here, you can click on any drawing and add memories from the day by clicking "edit associations" at the bottom. You can describe the day in writing on the left. On the right, you can add images (hosted on https://imgbb.com/) and videos/music (from YouTube) via their links - simply separate the different links with a single space character. Save and close - your memories now exist externally from you!

If you haven't logged in with a username & password that I have given to you, you won't be able to actually save anything.
