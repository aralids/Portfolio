How to use this app:

To document a day, the very least you have to do is draw a symbol - even if it's a little baby dot in case you don't feel like drawing. For that, click the 'drawing mode' button, choose a color from the palette on the left, and draw. What color was your day, and what shape? (By default your drawing will be associated with today, but you can choose any other day, top center.) Don't forget to save!

Once you're done, you can switch back to 'viewing mode'. Here, you can click on any drawing, view your memories from the day it was drawn, and edit them by clicking the button at the bottom. You can describe it in text on the left. On the right, you can add images (host them on https://imgbb.com/) and videos/music (from youtube) via their links - simply separate the different links with a single space character. Save, reload - your memories have been immortalized!
